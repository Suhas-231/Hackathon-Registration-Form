Event Schedule

Discover the journey of the Hackathon from start to finish. Here's a detailed schedule for each day, so you know what to expect.

Day 1: Inauguration & Keynote

09:00 AM - Registration & Welcome

Participants check in, collect welcome kits, and network with fellow hackers.

10:00 AM - Opening Ceremony

Kick-off the Hackathon with inspiring speeches from industry leaders and sponsors.

11:00 AM - Keynote Address

Keynote speech by a prominent guest speaker sharing insights on innovation and creativity.

12:00 PM - Networking & Team Formation

Meet other participants, discuss ideas, and form teams for the Hackathon challenge.

Day 2: Workshops & Development

09:00 AM - Technical Workshop

Hands-on session on cutting-edge technologies and development tools.

12:00 PM - Lunch Break

Take a break and enjoy some delicious food with fellow participants.

01:00 PM - Coding Begins

Teams start working on their projects and receive guidance from mentors.

04:00 PM - Feedback Session

Participants receive feedback from mentors on their progress.

Day 3: Hackathon & Midnight Coding

09:00 AM - Project Refinement

Participants continue to refine and develop their projects with mentor guidance.

01:00 PM - Lunch Break

Refuel yourself and prepare for the crucial final coding stretch.

03:00 PM - Hackathon Continues

Teams work through the night to bring their ideas to life.

10:00 PM - Midnight Motivation

Late-night motivational session to keep spirits high during the overnight coding marathon.

Day 4: Project Submission & Closing

07:00 AM - Final Submission

Teams submit their final project builds for review by the judges.

09:00 AM - Project Demos

Teams present their projects to judges, receive feedback, and share their journey.

12:00 PM - Closing Ceremony

Award announcements, special mentions, and the closing of the Hackathon.

HackForChange: Building a Better Tomorrow, One Website at a Time

This year, HackForChange continues to push the boundaries of what a hackathon can be. Beyond the adrenaline of coding sprints, the thrill of competition, and the pursuit of recognition, it’s about something far more profound...

At HackForChange, you’re stepping into a community that believes in the power of open innovation — not for the sake of winning, but for the love of impactful creation. From developing platforms for education access to building tools that support mental health, every website you create has the potential to change lives for the better.
